Blue Asylum is an underwater jail. As the main character you wake up in a dark cell, and your first step will be to get out of it. Once done, you'll have to explore the building and find clues to solve the puzzles you'll find and scape the Asylum.

Project Asteroids

In this game you are the tool of an AI (Casiopea) that wants to use you to save its master. You will have to explore the world to find useful things to complete the AI demands, watching not to die of thirst, or avoiding obstacles.

Pay attention to what Casiopea has to say. She may be rude, but now and then she'll give you advice and will take care of her precious tool, you.
