Metabolikka Serious game for Android

The game is a Serious game in 2D based on Android. It was ordered by the Biology department at the "Universitat de Girona", and it was meant as a tool to help it's students to learn how some biochemical process work, in this case, the Krebs Cycle.

Metabolikka is just a demo of what was innitially designed. This demo includes a full level that brings the player through the 10 stages of the Krebs Cycle. In this level you'll have to move the molecule inside the cell to find an enzym to go to the next stage. During the level you'll find some obstacles, like other molecules or even poisons.

On itch.io you can find an .apk file to download on your Adroid device, but beware that your device can block it's installation if you don't trust on foreign files.
